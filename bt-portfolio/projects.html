Website Portfolio

The first project, I made my portfolio as an active resume and resource to hand to all interested in me. This portfolio will be updated as more work is added to it, and will continue changing and adapting as I hone my skills as a Web Developer. This project took roughly 52 hours of work to complete, and any feedback you happen to have regarding it is always appreciated. There were two iterations of my portfolio before this, and all are available upon request.
